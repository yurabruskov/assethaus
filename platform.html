How It Works

Four simple steps to tokenize and trade real estate assets.

Step 01

Asset Selection

Choose high-quality real estate assets for tokenization. Our team conducts thorough due diligence, legal review, and property valuation.

Step 02

Smart Contract Deployment

Deploy ERC-20 tokens representing fractional ownership. Smart contracts handle governance, revenue distribution, and compliance automatically.

Step 03

Token Issuance

Accredited investors purchase tokens through our platform. All transactions are recorded immutably on the blockchain.

Step 04

Secondary Trading

Token holders can trade on our marketplace or external exchanges. Rental income and appreciation are distributed automatically.
