Six-Step Process

01

Consultation & Asset Valuation

Analyze property from legal and financial perspectives. Determine tokenization opportunities, minimum investment, ownership structure, and jurisdiction selection.

02

Legal Documentation

Develop comprehensive documentation: emission terms, registration documents, investor agreements. Ensure regulatory compliance and complete legal due diligence. Establish KYC/AML partnerships.

03

Token Creation & Issuance

Technical implementation on chosen blockchain (Ethereum, Polygon, BSC). Issue security tokens representing fractional ownership. Integrate smart contracts for rights distribution, dividends, and governance.

04

Investor Platform Launch

Create investor portal with complete transparency: investment opportunities, documents, investment status. Automate KYC, onboarding, and digital document signing. Ensure user-friendly investor experience.

05

Fundraising & Investor Attraction

Organize marketing campaigns, webinars, roadshows. Engage with partner networks, institutional and retail investors. Post-placement support: portfolio management, reporting, secondary market.

06

Asset Management & Aftercare

Automate dividend and income distribution. Ensure ownership transparency and data updates. Support token resale and investment exit processes through secondary market infrastructure.
